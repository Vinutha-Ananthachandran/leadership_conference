Speaker

Dr.Trina Gregory is an Associate Professor of Information Technology Practice teaching technology, programming, and engineering courses. She has been teaching at USC for 16 years. She created and oversees the Mobile App Development minor. She is also an Associate Director of Innovation and Entrepreneurship for the Viterbi Office of Technology Innovation and Entrepreneurship.

After earning a bachelor's degree in Mathematics-Computer Science from UCSD, Dr.Gregory spent 12 years working full-time in the computer science industry. During this time, she primarily worked for SAIC, where she implemented software and managed technology projects for the Department of Defense.

Dr.Gregory received a Masters degree in Computer Science from USC. She is currently pursuing an Ed.D. from the Rossier School of Education in Organizational Change and Leadership.

Dr.Gregory has taught classes in web publishing, information technology, and mobile app development, as well as programming courses in Java, Python, Swift, and Objective-C. She strives to continuously update course materials to keep them up-to-date with the latest technologies and methodologies.

She has a passion for helping students achieve their goals and is involved in various student organizations
