We Can Do It - Boeing Women's Panel

Location

Champion Ballroom
USC Hotel

3540 South Figueroa Street

Los Angeles, CA 90007

Speaker

Diana Lopez is a BDS Command and Control Systems Capability Senior Manager at the Boeing Company where she began her career is 2002 and has remained at for the past 19 years.

She earned her Bachelors of Science at USC in Electrical Engineering in 2003 and Masters of Science in Information Systems and Technology at Claremont Graduate University. In her field, her main expertise is in information and communication systems, networks, programs and project execution.

At Boeing, she is a part of the Command and Control (C2) capability team where she is focused on identifying and supporting projects that continue to contribute to the C2 warfighter efforts. Apart from being a leader in her field, she greatly enjoys the process of creating and executing plans in pursuit of dreams, as well as enjoying her morning runs, weight-lifting and practicing the art of yoga.

Laurel Gutierrez is a Senior Director of Command and Control Systems Capability at the Boeing Company in the Defense, Space, and Security sector. She earned her Bachelors of Science in Applied Mathematics from UCLA and her Masters of Science in Systems Architecting and Engineering from USC in 1993.

In her field, her main expertise is in aerospace systems engineering. In her current role she leads a business-wide team focused on engineering capabilities in the command and control mission area.

Apart from her outstanding career as an engineer, she enjoys helping people reach their potential, creating innovative concepts and solutions, and in her spare time she enjoys cycling, playing the violin, and traveling.

Cynthia Gonzalez Chaparro is a Electrical Design and Analysis Engineer at the Boeing Company where she started her career in 2021 after earning her Bachelors of Science in Physics from the University of California, Riverside in 2020.

Her expertise is in electrical engineering, electrical testing, power, and digital electronics. In her current role, she is responsible for engineering authority at the unit level. She supports her units from initial build through test. In order to so do, she has to understand circuit design requirements, troubleshoot non conformances and make sure they are delivering functioning product at unit levels.

Apart from being a major contributor on her team, she loves being in nature and greatly enjoys hiking, going to the beach, and listening to music. However, she also likes challenging herself, advocating for women in STEM, and representing her community.

Maida Lopez is currently a Space and Launch System Engineer at the Boeing Company where she began her early career 19 years ago and been a systems engineer there since 2003 in the early career rotation program. She earned her Bachelors of Science in Electrical Engineering at Cal State Los Angeles in 2003 and her Masters of Science in Information Systems and Technology from Claremont Graduate University.

Her expertise is in the development of core infrastructures, system engineering, integration, network systems, mission, information security, and system tests. In her role, she is responsible for the validation and verification of systems (End-to-End).

Apart from being a visionary engineering and leader with a proven track record, she enjoys spending time with family and friends. She also enjoys supporting outreach activities. She recently started exploring new hobbies and one of which is paddling on the weekends in Long Beach.
